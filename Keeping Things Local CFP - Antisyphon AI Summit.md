### Promo Blurb
Ever wanted your own private version of ChatGPT? LLMs offer powerful capabilities, but not everyone is comfortable sending their data over the internet to Microsoft, OpenAI, or Anthropic. Fortunately, open-source tools make it possible to set up and customize a local, secure LLM on your own terms. From installation to optimization, this workshop will guide you through the process step by step, with demos to illustrate each stage. It’s time to build your own mini-Jarvis and start getting things done, efficiently, securely, and privately.

### Detailed Outline
This is a new presentation I am creating in response to questions, comments, and feedback from people who have attended or watched previous talks I have given about LLMs and prompt engineering. One of the biggest concerns cybersecurity professionals have is about sending data, especially client data, to third parties like Microsoft, OpenAI, etc. This talk will specifically address those issues by showing people how to install, run, and customize LLMs on a local system.

The rough outline atm is:

* Brief review of LLMs and their models
* Installing Ollama
* Using Ollama locally - CLI and web GUI
* Creating a custom model
* Daffy Duck (for PoC)
* More robust model - maybe something tooled for GRC

That's as far as I have gotten so far. Feel free to reach out if you have any questions, requests, etc.

Also, even though I selected the 60 min training category, I am willing to go for a 120 min workshop if you want/need. There is PLENTY of material to cover and the longer window would give us more time to play.
